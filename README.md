#Dr.Dot

Dr. Dot is an open-source suite of tools and commands on the Amazon Echo to allow people to get matched to the medical services they need as soon as possible. Through different commands, we wish to be able to help people of all ages and occupations, whether it be children, adults, or the elderly, find and contact the correct medical services for their specific situations.

Some features in place currently are:
  * Contacts caregivers and emergency contacts in times of emergency through text using Twilio's API Service
  * Gives you information on a doctor in the area who can take care of the specific illness symptopms you are experiencing
